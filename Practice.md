# 📱 English Practice: Prep for Tomorrow!
## 1. Past Tenses (Simple vs. Continuous vs. Perfect)
**Rule:** Use *Past Continuous* for the background, *Past Simple* for the action, and *Past Perfect* for things that happened even earlier.

**Task:** While I **(1. walk)** to the park, I realized I **(2. leave)** my bag at the station. I **(3. go)** back immediately.

<details>
<summary>✅ Check Answers</summary>
1. was walking <br>
2. had left <br>
3. went
</details>
